## Results Tables

This project produces a total of **five empirical result tables**, corresponding to the main analyses in the paper:

1. **Baseline Regression Results**  
   - `regression_results.csv`  
   - `regression_results.txt`  
   Main fixed-effects regression estimates for the baseline specification.

2. **Lag and Lead (Causality) Analysis**  
   - `regression_results_causal.csv`  
   - `regression_results_causal.txt`  
   Regression results with lag and lead terms to examine potential reverse causality.

3. **Robustness Checks**  
   - `robustness_results.csv`  
   - `robustness_results.txt`  
   Robustness tests including alternative specifications and sample exclusions.

4. **Heterogeneity Analysis**  
   - `heterogeneity_results.csv`  
   - `heterogeneity_results.txt`  
   Subsample and interaction-based heterogeneity analysis across regions and economic structures.

5. **Marginal Effects Analysis**  
   - `marginal_effects_fintech.csv`  
   Estimated marginal effects and turning-point analysis for fintech development.
